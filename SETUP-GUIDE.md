# OPEN SEA TCG - Setup Guide

## Business Setup Checklist

### 1. LLC Status (EST88 - Maryland)

**Check Status**: [MD Business Search](https://egov.maryland.gov/BusinessExpress/EntitySearch)

| Status | Action | Cost |
|--------|--------|------|
| Active | Good to go | - |
| Forfeited | File reinstatement | $100-$150 |

**Reinstatement Form**: [SDAT Reinstatement PDF](https://dat.maryland.gov/sdat%20forms/reinstatement.pdf)

**Contact**: SDAT.charterhelp@maryland.gov | (410) 767-1184

---

### 2. Register as Foreign LLC in California

Since you're operating in CA with a MD LLC:

- **File**: Foreign LLC Registration with CA Secretary of State
- **Cost**: ~$70 filing fee
- **Annual**: $800 CA Franchise Tax (required for ALL CA LLCs)

**File Here**: [CA SOS Foreign Qualification](https://www.sos.ca.gov/business-programs/business-entities/foreign-qualification)

---

### 3. California Seller's Permit / Resale Certificate

**Apply Online**: [CDTFA Online Services](https://onlineservices.cdtfa.ca.gov/_/)

**Steps**:
1. Register → "Register a New Business Activity"
2. Get Seller's Permit (often instant)
3. Download [CDTFA-230 Resale Certificate](https://cdtfa.ca.gov/formspubs/cdtfa230.pdf)

**What You Need**:
- LLC/Business info + EIN
- SSN
- Business address
- Estimated monthly sales

**Phone Support**: 1-800-400-7115 (Mon-Fri 7:30am-5pm PT)

### 6. What to Say in Applications

**DO SAY**:
> "We are a sealed-product focused TCG retailer with strong demand for One Piece. We are interested in case quantities and consistent allocations."

**Keywords**: Retail, Community demand, Long-term purchasing

**DON'T SAY**: Reseller, Flip, "I just want One Piece"

---

### 7. Allocation Reality Check

**Expect This Timeline**:
- First orders: Loose boxes, small quantities
- Cases: After trust + volume built
- Fast-track: Buy Digimon/DBS, pay invoices immediately, never miss preorders

**While Waiting**:
- Local game stores (case cash deals)
- Secondary wholesalers
- Networking at Bandai events
